  • Climatique

    Climatique

    In 2023, I helped create a community group to raise awareness of the climate crisis. We called ourselves Climatique, and we quickly built momentum. We’re now a registered nonprofit who holds weekly community gatherings, serving free regeneratively farmed meals and educating people on the skills they need to create a sustainable world. I’ve had the pleasure of developing our branding and designing posters, zines, pamphlets and more.

    One of our first projects was a zine to introduce folks to basic concepts around sustainability, community organizing and the polycrisis.


    Me and my comrades at Climatique worked alongside another Boulder nonprofit, Woven Web, to build out a conceptual map of the different Boulder nonprofits. The goal was to create a tool for newcomers to changemaking and activism, something both rich enough to be useful and legible enough to be approachable.

    The map is still a work in progress, but I'm quite proud of the current version. You can see a full-sized PDF here.

    My favorite project so far with Climatique has been designing stickers, illustrating some key messages in creative ways.

  • Carbon Pardon
    •

    Carbon Pardon

    Carbon Pardon is a tongue-in-cheek climate change awareness campaign. Inspired by catholic indulgences, the website offers physical or digital "pardons" which absolve users of their greenhouse emission "sins."

    I designed the Carbon Pardon website and the paper pardons, which are made to order on seed paper from a local Boulder business! Proceeds go to hand-picked reforestation projects.
